Blue Tit

The Blue tit (Cyanistes caeruleus) is a small passerine bird in the tit family, Paridae. They are easily recognisable by thier blue and yellow plumage and small size. Blue tits are non-migratory birds found commonly in European woodslands and prefer insects, spiders and seeds for their diet.

The birds are famed for their skill, as they can cling to the outermost branches and hang upside down when looking for food.
